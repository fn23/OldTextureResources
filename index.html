Old Texture Resource

Much of the tutorials and resources focusing on older styles of texturing, have been lost after people have moved on to more modern methods like PBR texturing and simply using Substance Painter. Which have resulted in such websites no longer being maintained and simply being removed off of the internet. While website archivers have been a thing for more than a few decades, it has resulted in incomplete archives of many of these resources, with much of this information lost.

This site will try to act as an archive of this content no longer available, while also linking to some resources still currently online.

If anyone has any backups or copies of this content from any of the sites offline below or other similar resources, it would be great if you could contribute to this here!
